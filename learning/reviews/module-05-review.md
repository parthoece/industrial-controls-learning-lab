# Module 5 review — Smart manufacturing

Answer without opening the weekly lessons.

1. Choose OPC UA, MQTT, or REST for machine browsing.

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

OPC UA is the typical fit because of typed information models and services.

</details>

2. Why is application idempotency required with retries?

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

Transport can redeliver; the business operation must reject duplicate IDs.

</details>

3. What fields support part genealogy?

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

Part ID, event ID, event type/time, asset/station, cycle/work-order/recipe links, and sequence/order.

</details>

4. Why must OEE assumptions be published?

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

Planned time, ideal cycle, reject/rework, and product mix definitions change the result.

</details>

5. Name four OT security controls.

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

Segmentation, least privilege, unique credentials, controlled remote access, backups, audit logs, or patch/change management.

</details>
